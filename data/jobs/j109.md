# Legal Specialist – Case Analysis & Statutes

**Location:** Belo Horizonte
**Type:** Full-Time

**About the Role:**
We are seeking a **Legal Specialist** to join our team in Belo Horizonte. You'll focus on analyzing legal cases and interpreting statutory requirements. This position requires solid analytical skills and foundational knowledge of legal research methods.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Review and analyze legal cases to identify relevant precedents and patterns
- Research statutory requirements and regulatory frameworks
- Prepare detailed case summaries and legal documentation
- Support legal proceedings with thorough case preparation
- Maintain organized case files and legal research databases
- Collaborate with legal team members on complex matters

**Qualifications:**
- Tecnólogo degree required
- 1 year of experience in legal analysis or related field
- Intermediate-level experience in case review and legal analysis
- Basic understanding of statutory interpretation principles
- Fluency in Portuguese (Brazilian)

**Preferred Qualifications:**
- Experience with legal research databases
- Knowledge of Brazilian legal system and procedures

**How to Apply:**
Please submit your resume and cover letter detailing your relevant legal experience.
